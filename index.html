My Background

My name is Esther Wangari Mureithi ,a frontend developer and UI/UX designer based in Nairobi,Kenya.I have a four year experience in designing websites by use of JavaScript,HTMl , CSS and bootstrap skills that i learnt and acquired in Moringa School. The companies i have worked for so far are Techworld ,MicroVerse and I am now looking forward to partnering with Google and Microsoft. I am fueled by my passion for coding and designig websites , a passion I have had since i was young and that is i decided to pursure my dreams. What I am most passionate about in designing websites is the fact that I am able to challenge my creativity and also I am doing what i love and I as result my work is top tier and world class.
